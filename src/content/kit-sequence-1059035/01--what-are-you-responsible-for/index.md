Your first task is to figure out what you are responsible for. Are you responsible for **People**, **Projects**, **Process**, or **Technical** decisionmaking? Try to figure out what the standard approach is at your company. If you're not sure, talk with your manager about it this week.

Based on what you discover, you'll know which emails from this course are most relevant to you. If you're not responsible for project management, for example, you can skip any of the posts on project management. (It still might be good to read them, but you won't have to worry about implementing them)
