Do a deep dive on how the team works.

Look at how teams around you work. Are there similar teams you can learn from?

Take an inventory of who is good at what around you.

​

Write down your team's process.

When you make changes, update the team's process. (It's not official until it's in the wiki)

Process should be fluid, adapting.

When you write down process for something, include context and rationale. How you would know to update it. Give yourself (or a future manager) enough info so they can know what parts of the process are core, and which are flexible.
